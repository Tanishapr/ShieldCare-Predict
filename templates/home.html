About Us

We are a team of college students passionate about using technology to make life easier. Our project, ShieldCare Predict, helps individuals predict the cost of their health insurance based on various factors like age, gender, BMI, and more. We aim to empower people with the knowledge they need to make informed decisions about their health insurance.
